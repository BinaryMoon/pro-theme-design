Website Security and a New Theme

April 24th, 2013

Security, Security, Security

Recently there have been a number of attacks on WordPress websites. Last week hackers used a botnet (a collection of servers) to attack many WordPress powered sites trying to force their way in. And recently a variety of security flaws have been found in popular WordPress plugins. I go over it in more detail here - but it boils down to the fact that you must keep WordPress and your plugins up to date.

For the recent botnet attack I recommend the Limit Login Attempts plugin - but there are all sorts of other plugins that will help too. Whatever you decide though - make sure to keep security at the front of your mind.
